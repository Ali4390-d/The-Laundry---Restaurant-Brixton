Welcome to Brixton

Brixton's Hidden Gem

Good food, natural wine, zero pretension.

Nestled in the vibrant heart of Brixton, The Laundry is an all-day bistro and wine shop born from a love of seasonal ingredients and relaxed dining. What started as a local laundering facility has been transformed into a warm, industrial-chic haven for food lovers.

We serve uncomplicated, delicious food from morning till night, paired with an eclectic selection of natural and low-intervention wines. Whether you're grabbing a quick coffee, lingering over brunch, or enjoying a late-night glass, you're always welcome here.
